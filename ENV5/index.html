Environment 5: A nation committed to ensuring the conservation and sustainable management of our biodiversity and ecosystems

Policy objectives

Indicators

ENV5.1 Protect biodiversity and ecosystems and their significant role in our culture, society and environment
ENV5.1.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of NBSAP and NISSAP targets met
ENV5.1.2 Exploring data sources Total number of environmental guidelines in place
ENV5.2 Create and manage conservation and protected areas
ENV5.2.1 Exploring data sources Total number of registered CCAs
ENV5.2.2 Exploring data sources Proportion of community management committees implementing their CCA management plan
ENV5.3 Support local conservation and protection of endangered, threatened or endemic species and ecosystems including through traditional knowledge and practices
ENV5.3.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of population with knowledge of the names of local flora and fauna
ENV5.3.2 Exploring data sources Number of threatened species legally protected
ENV5.4 Protect our borders and environment through effective customs and biosecurity services
ENV5.4.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of incoming planes and sea vessels cleared through Biosecurity annually
ENV5.5 Increase awareness on biodiversity conservation and environmental protection issues across government and publicly
ENV5.5.1 Exploring data sources Number of model schools implementing environmental programmes
ENV5.6 Enhance environmental monitoring, evaluation and research with relevant, open and transparent data sharing among relevant agencies
ENV5.6.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of government agencies using a central information sharing system for environment data
